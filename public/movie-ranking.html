My favorite Anime of Ghibli Studio

My top 3 anime of all-time

The Cat Returns

The movie is about a girl who saved a cat, which turned out to be the prince of a cat kingdom. To repay her, the king—father of the cat prince—decided to marry his son to the girl. However, the cat prince did not want to get married, and if the girl married him, she would also turn into a cat. So, she tried to escape from that cat kingdom.

What happens in the movie is very interesting, and I absolutely love the soundtrack of this film.

OST: 風になる

Tales from Earthsea

The story is about a prince whose body and soul were taken over by a dark force. He tried to fight against it but failed, so he decided to leave the palace and find a way to save himself. He truly found himself again when he met that girl. However, she carried wounds that could never fade. When they met, they healed each other.

OST: Therru's Song (てるの歌)

Whisper of the Heart

The movie tells the story of a girl in her teenage years (14-15 years old) who is trying to find herself—who she is and what she wants to do.

OST: Country Road - Japanses
